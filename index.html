Introduction

  

  
In Formula 1, achieving peak performance on a circuit requires an understanding of the "optimal racing line," which is the trajectory that minimizes lap times, this line is influenced by aerodynamic forces, tire grip, weight distribution and track conditions. Understanding the racing line involves applying principles from physics including, equations of friction, motion and centripetal forces as well as analysing real-world data.
